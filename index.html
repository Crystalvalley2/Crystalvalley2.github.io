[About]

Keri Griggs is a playwright and audio scriptwriter based in Utah. Her most recently staged 10-minute play "That's the Spirit" placed second in the Mayhew Playwriting Competition in 2025, and was selected to participate in the 2026 Region 8 National Playwriting Program as part of the American College Theatre Festival. Two of her audio dramas will be featured on BYUradio's Kaboom Podcast in 2026. She is currently studying Theatre Arts Studies with an emphasis in playwriting and a minor in creative writing
